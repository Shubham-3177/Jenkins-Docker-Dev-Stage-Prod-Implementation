What we do
Surprise.com is on a mission to turn the magical, uplifting experience
of Surprise into a daily, weekly, and monthly life event – because it
makes people happy.

Our Surprise Box, and delightful companion app, is the most exciting
way to get amazing goodies from brands you love and brands you’re
about to love. With daily games, the ability to send and get Surprises
from friends, and Grand Surprises – every day is filled with wow!




Surprise Team
We are an international team with Silicon Valley roots, headquartered
in the US with R&D offices in Europe. At Surprise.com, people work in
self-directed teams, driven by challenging tasks and opportunities
to grow, create, innovate and reach their full potential.

We are already over 150 ambitious top-performers, most of them are
in software development, and we’re always looking to grow it. Join us
to trade best practices with fellow innovators and thought leaders from
across the globe!




Surprise Culture
Surprise is an arena for minds and hearts, not just a place to clock in
and out. A company of builders and thinkers, we appreciate radical
truth, wise decisions, and the ability to modify actions on the fly.

We provide a high-performance culture that favors action over talk and
collective achievement over self-defeat. This is a great place for people
who can make things happen, who don’t just follow instructions but
accept daily tasks as challenges!.
